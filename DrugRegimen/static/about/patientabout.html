RegX is a service which allows doctors to manage perscriptions for their patients. It facilitates pharmacies coupling prescriptions with QR codes which the patient scans to inform their doctor and to check for medical conflicts.

Our service offers a range of services for patients, including the following:

Intuitive and universally accepted paperless presciptions

Accountability on keeping on top of prescriptions          

Full access to interactive database of drug interactions
